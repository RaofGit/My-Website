Frequently Asked Questions

Do these packages cover the FULL bike?

Yes! Every package covers your full motorcycle. The price differences are solely due to the raw material cost (PVC vs TPH vs pure TPU).

What is the difference between Standard and Premium?

Standard uses TPH which has mild self-healing. Premium uses 100% Pure TPU, which heals deeper scratches automatically under sunlight and gives a far deeper gloss.

How long does the installation take?

A full bike PPF installation typically takes between 4 to 6 hours depending on the complexity of your bike's fairings and curves.

Do I have to pay an advance fee?

No, we operate on a strict "No Advance Payment" policy. You only pay once the job is completed and you are satisfied with the result.
