Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a high-pressure wash on my bike after PPF?

Yes, you can! However, we recommend keeping the spray nozzle at least 12-18 inches away from the wrapped edges to ensure long-lasting adhesion.

What is the difference between PVC and TPU film?

PVC is cheap plastic that turns yellow, cracks, and ruins paint within months. We exclusively use imported TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane), which is genuinely self-healing, crystal-clear, and highly stretchable.

Does it leave glue residue behind when removed?

Not at all. Our premium quality films feature safe automotive-grade acrylic adhesives that pull off cleanly without damaging your original factory paint even after years.
